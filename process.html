Our Process

Typically our process includes 5 steps:
understanding, storytelling, design, animation and delivering.

If you already have a strong vision on the future result, or you have an existing video with styles that have to be followed, or you have ready voiceover text or structure of the animated video,
then 01 and 02 steps will be modified and it can save time a little bit.

Step 1: Understanding

We get to know and listen to each other, ask questions and answer in detail.

  • Talking & chatting to better understand the task.
  • Defining main goals and needs.
  • Collecting existing materials and thoughts.
  • Searching for solution options and appropriate examples.

Step 2: Story

This phase needs a very tight collaboration and synergy.

  • Defining the skeleton of the story: what for start and how to finish.
  • Proposing a few high-level ideas with mood boards or examples.
  • Collaborative writing a text. Usually, any proposed text will be rewritten by the client, because no one can know your product better than you. And it's OK.

Step 3: Design

We start to play with fonts, colors, and sizes.

  • Drawing & designing a full set of scenes.
  • You choose a voice, we record a voiceover.
  • Animatic: video with voiceover, music and static scenes to feel future animation dynamics. Usually, that step can indicate some gaps in the timing.

Very important to have an approved voiceover at the very beginning of working on the animation — making changes to the timing of the animation is time-consuming and can affect the deadline and cost.

Step 4: Animation

This phase is the longest, always needs more time and will check your patience skills.

It's time for an animation routine. We are always trying to define reusable elements to speed-up process, but don't forget that from the other side — each project is unique. Keep calm and wait!

Step 5: Delivery

When animation complete, we finalize music and start to work on sound effects. Also, you can ask to prepare different versions for different needs or different timing with different endings, etc - it needs additional time for editing and mixing. By the way, we in love with sound effects, because they make animated elements alive, therefore, at every opportunity, we will refine them in parallel with other processes.

We can deliver not only video files. Typically, we are preparing an additional set of audio tracks (voice, music, SFX) and folder with a set of key scenes in vector format for your future using.
