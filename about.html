Hello, we are Smooozy.

The main areas of our activity are design and animation. We provide the full cycle of creating an animated video - from the idea to the finished video voiced by a professional narrator. We are working remotely and undertake only those projects where there is an opportunity to make high quality and tasty.

We are a small team: there is a designer (me) and a few freelance animators. We can remotely involve copywriters who can write you several script options. Also, we are cooperating with a recording studio that can provide us records of speakers from around the world with any language. Sometimes we can take a project with a real video shooting where animation is needed, but still, the main direction of our activity is 2d-animation. That's why for projects with the need for real camera shooting we'll recommend professional local teams as: kantora, dimensions, and action.

Script and Storyboard

We can provide a detailed script with our vision on the text that you can update with your specific formulation or existing marketing text. For better understanding and imagining the future video we are creating a storyboard.

Illustrations and Design

For each of our projects, we are creating a set of illustrations and design: working with fonts, colors, proportions, etc. But also we can create a design only if you don’t need animation yet.

Animation

Each of our animation we are trying to make as a challenge: try a new technic or technology, to use not a typical approach, etc. And as the animation is our core activity — we spend a lot of time to make it natural, smooth and dynamic.

Voiceover and Sound Effects

We work with a studio that provides high-quality records of professional native speakers from around the world with any language. In the end, we'll add special sound effects to make non-real abstract objects live.

Get In Touch.

We aren't a regular studio with office, meeting room and coffee machine — we are a small team and working remotely. But we are open to communication online or to meet locally in the Lviv.
