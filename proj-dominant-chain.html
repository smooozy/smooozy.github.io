Dominant Chain

Dominant Chain is a new blockchain crypto-empowered tournament platform that is intended to improve the world of eSports. This product was formed with the following goal — to become the optimum platform for gaming and cryptocurrency, which will enable monetization of gaming time to players of any level, both alone and in the team. In other words, it’s a tournament for everyone!
